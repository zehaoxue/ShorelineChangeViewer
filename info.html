Texas Heritage Trails Map

This interactive map shows the Texas Heritage Trails for each Texas Historical Commission Regions, along with locations for breweries, wineries, and BBQ stands.

Map Controls Overview

Full Extent: Zooms to all visible data
Regions: Shows THC Regions
Trails: Shows Texas Heritage Trails
Wineries: Shows wineries
Breweries: Shows breweries
BBQ: Shows BBQ stands
Location: Zooms to current location
Basemaps: Toggles between basemap layers
Menu: Opens menu containing additional options
